Overview:

This module equips the aspiring leader to support teachers in developing and implementing the right strategies to support struggling leaners in their math instruction aligned to College and Career Readiness Standards.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Articulate non-negotiable instructional strategies to increase student achievement for struggling learners & English Language Learning students.
  2. Guide teachers in using CCRS Aligned math resources/tools to use when developing and implementing instructional strategies that support all learners. (2.1b)
