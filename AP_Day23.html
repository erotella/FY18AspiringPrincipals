Overview:

This module focuses on how to use communication strategies effectively for observation/feedback and curriculum conversations with teachers. It also prepares participants to introduce issues of equity that may surface in instructional practices of teachers.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Utilize coaching conversations as post-observation feedback methodology to elicit practice shifts as well as communicate expectations sensitively yet effectively. (2.3a, 2.4a)
  2. Use coaching conversations to engage in discovery with staff, to surface social and racial biases, and establish culturally responsive practices for equitable student outcomes. (3.2a)
