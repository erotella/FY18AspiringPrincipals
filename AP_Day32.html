Overview:

This module prepares the participant to approach large portions of strategic work that must be considered and structured in preparation for opening the school year successfully as principal.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Articulate the value and key components of an entry plan to provide vision, structure and direction for a newly assigned principal (1.3a)
  2. Collaboratively develop an entry plan, that includes effective practices that support teaching and learning, systems and structures, safety , and assessments to measure progress and adjustments (5.1c)
  3. Use an entry plan to operationalize the school’s mission and vision to achieve the district and school’s academic, climate and operational goals (3.3b)
