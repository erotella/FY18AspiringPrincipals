OVERVIEW:

This module focuses on how to use communication strategies effectively for observation/feedback and curriculum conversations with teachers. It also prepares participants to introduce issues of equity that may surface in instructional practices of teachers.

SESSION OUTCOMES:

  • Use coaching conversations as a post-observation feedback methodology to create instructional practice shifts. (2.3a)
  • Use coaching conversation constructs to communicate expectations sensitively yet effectively. (2.3a, 2.4a)
  • Use coaching conversations to engage in discovery with staff, to surface social and racial biases, and establish culturally responsive practices for equitable student outcomes. (3.2a)
