OVERVIEW:

This module is locally driven and designed to meet the needs of district and state requirements for local teacher evaluation systems. This session will equip participants with balancing observation and evaluation responsibilities.

SESSION OUTCOMES:

  • Describe ways in which an effective system of teacher observation and feedback can:
    1. Build momentum toward accomplishing a school’s mission (1.3b);
    2. Establish a strong sense of teacher urgency and efficacy in improving instructional practices (3.1c); and
    3. Enhance a leader’s ability to identify and interrupt inequitable systems and structures that students experience in schools. (3.2b)
  • Use research-based best practices in observing and providing effective feedback to teachers so as to incrementally improve instruction with each interaction. (2.3a)
  • Investigate the legal parameters that distinguish teacher evaluation from teacher observation, & identify strategic steps to be used to help navigate between the two. (2.1b)
