OVERVIEW:

This module allows participants to look at what goes into the design of coherent professional learning experiences as a result of using various data streams including trends found in coaching conversations and observations of teacher practices.

SESSION OUTCOMES:

  • A. Use multiple streams of both student and teacher data to identify and strategically shift trends in school-wide practices. (2.4b, 3.2b)
  • Use multiple streams of both teacher and student data to inform and design high-quality and relevant school-wide PD. (4.3a)
