Overview:

This module allows participants to look at what goes into the design of coherent professional learning experiences as a result of using various data streams including trends found in coaching conversations and observations of teacher practices.

Session Outcomes:

  1. Utilize multiple streams of both student and teacher data to confirm trends in schoolwide practices, and support the creation of equitable school cultures that support learning. (2.4b, 3.2b)
  2. Utilizing multiple streams of both teacher and student data to inform and design high-quality and relevant school-wide PD. (4.3a)
